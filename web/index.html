What Is This?

Scirtothrips dorsalis (Chilli Thrips) — a tiny insect pest that feeds on tender leaves and flower buds. Causes leaf curl, silvering, and flower drop. Can reduce yield by 30–50% if untreated.

Symptoms Found in Your Photos

✅ Upward leaf curling    ✅ Silver streaks on leaves
✅ Flower bud shedding    ⚠️ Early stage fruit damage
